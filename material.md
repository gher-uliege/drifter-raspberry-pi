# Materials for one drifter:

* 1 Raspberry Pi 4
* 1 [GSM/GPRS/GNSS/Bluetooth HAT for Raspberry Pi](https://www.waveshare.com/gsm-gprs-gnss-hat.htm)
* 1 SIM card (with credit)
* 1 Plastic box
* wooden beam
* wooden board
* fabric (for sails)
* 4 M8 threaded bars 
* 24 M8 screws
* 24 M8 washers

Inox is preferred for metal parts.
